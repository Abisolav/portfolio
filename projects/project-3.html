Project Overview

Calorie Creatures is a playful and motivational fitness app that transforms calories burned into points. The users use these points to hatch and collect virtual pets. Designed for casual movers and gym people, the app blends lighthearted visuals with real-world movement. It encourages users to stay active and track progress in a fun and rewarding way. The experience revolves around movement toreward mechanics, where every step, rep, or jump brings users closer to unlocking their next adorable companion.

My Role

I created the unique pet silhouettes and final creature designs that serve as the heart of the experience. Each pet begins as a mystery egg, paired with an outline that sparks curiosity and motivates the user to earn points through physical activity. I developed the progression system from egg to fully revealed pet, ensuring a sense of anticipation and delight. Working closely with the coder, I also helped define the core creature-collection loop and visual feedback system that encourages daily engagement and movement tracking.

Process

The design process began with user interviews and behavior mapping to understand what types of rewards motivate users in fitness apps. Early brainstorming sessions led to the egg and silhouette mechanic, which I illustrated through multiple concept sketches and mood boards. I iterated on over a dozen pet designs in Procreate and sketch, focusing on personality, cuteness, and variation. In parallel, I helped define the silhouette system to maintain mystery while giving just enough visual clues. Regular feedback from testers shaped the silhouettes‚Äô readability and the final pet animations, which were optimized for mobile responsiveness and emotional engagement.

Key Features

Calorie Creatures turns calorie burn into tangible in-app progress. Each egg is paired with a silhouette of a surprise creature users must earn enough points to reveal and hatch it. The app uses daily movement data to drive progression, supporting a variety of activity types. Creatures range in rarity and design style, keeping the collection dynamic and exciting. The UI emphasizes accessibility and charm, while motivational prompts and milestones reinforce daily fitness habits. New pets are added regularly to encourage long-term use.
