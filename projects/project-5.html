Presently Yours

Project Overview

Presently Yours was my entry for the Swift Student Challenge. Designed for anyone who struggles to communicate face to face, the app provides a safe, thoughtful space to record, save, and revisit heartfelt messages.

My Role

I designed and built the entire prototype solo—creating a warm UI in Sketch & Procreate and coding everything in SwiftUI with AVFoundation / AVFAudio. I also conducted accessibility and usability testing to ensure color-blind-friendly contrast and a stress-free flow.

Process

It started with really bad design due to broken code. the user testing with five target participants surfaced contrast issues that I corrected in later builds. After multiple polish passes texture compression tweaks, color grade adjustments, and input, the app was soft launched via TestFlight to gather telemetry and qualitative feedback.

Key Features

• Private voice-message recording without live-conversation pressure

• Securely saved conversations users can revisit or send later

• Gentle emotional prompts that help articulate feelings

• Soothing, minimal visual design for calm reflection

• Accessibility-focused UI (high contrast, dyslexia-friendly fonts, color-blind palettes)

Outcomes and Impact

Testers called the app “therapeutic” and “emotionally freeing.” One remarked, “This is the first time I’ve ever said these words out loud, and it felt safe.” Presently Yours was featured in the 2025 Swift Student Challenge Showcase for blending empathy with solid technical execution.

Next Steps

I planned to updates and include optional message delivery, mood-based themes, and a text-journaling mode. These features will deepen user trust and broaden the ways people can share what matters.
