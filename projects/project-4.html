Project Overview

Hopps Alpha is the evolved version of the original Hopps. It's a vertical 2D platformer reimagined with a layered parallax world, infinite scrolling, and animated transitions that take the player from ground to galaxy. This updated version offers a more immersive visual experience and deeper player progression. Inspired by Celeste, Downwell, and Doodle Jump, Hopps Alpha combines intuitive controls with dynamic environments and unlockable character skins to keep players engaged through every climb.

My Role

I directed the transformation from the original Hopps to Hopps Alpha. I redefined the visual theme and progression system to support the new world-to-space concept. I illustrated and animated the background parallax layers to create depth and motion. I implemented an endless scroll system for a continuous gameplay experience. I also worked on expanding character design, adding unlockable skins to personalize gameplay. My role included level theming, motion design, and performance optimization to ensure smooth transitions and efficient loading between zones.

Process

We began by mapping the vertical journey across multiple visual zones, Earth, Sky, and Galaxy — and developed a parallax framework for each. I created modular background assets that animated independently, giving each stage a distinct sense of movement and place. We integrated sprite animations, transition effects, and randomized elements to make each session feel fresh. Weekly playtests helped us adjust visual speed, parallax depth, and animation pacing for a balance between aesthetics and clarity. I also tested different skin implementation systems and finalized a smooth unlock mechanism linked to in-game score milestones.

Key Features

Blast into adventure, as you climb higher with each jump—your altitude and score unlock visually stunning new layers of the game world. As you ascend, the environment seamlessly transforms: platforms, backgrounds, and color palettes shift from urban skylines to galactic heights, creating a sense of momentum and wonder. Enemies feature dynamic omni-directional movement, adding an unpredictable challenge to every run and forcing players to react with precision and timing. Skins can be collected and worn in-game, allowing players to express themselves as they play.

Outcomes and Impact

With 50 players and counting, a significant higher engagement was reported, with session times increasing by 75% compared to the original Hopps. The Visual updates like animated parallax, layers and unlockable skins were frequently praised in feedback, along with the seamless transition system. The performance remained stable despite added complexity, thanks to draw-call optimization and efficient sprite handling. Hopps Alpha was showcase at the 2025 apple developper academy as one of the best casual game on the appstore.

Next Steps

Our next milestones include adding a wider variety of collectible skins, introducing new platform types and enemy behaviors to increase challenge and replayability, and designing additional game scene themes to enrich the vertical journey. We’re also exploring options to monetize the app responsibly, through optional cosmetic upgrades and rewards that enhance the experience without interrupting gameplay.
