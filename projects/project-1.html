Madame Coffee

Project Overview

Madame Coffee is a personalized coffee recommendation app that not only suggests a daily coffee choice but also provides a positive affirmation to start your day on a refreshing note. During the design process, I focused on crafting an elegant and engaging user experience, ensuring that the app is not only functional but also visually appealing.

My user Andrea is a coffee connoisseur who enjoys exploring new coffee flavors. She often finds herself overwhelmed by the multitude of coffee options available. The app aims to simplify her decision-making process by providing personalized recommendations based on her preferences and mood. The app also includes detailed instructions and ideal food pairings for each coffee choice. here is a diagram of the user flow

Design and Development Approach

Madame Coffee started with a soft pink on white background, Andrea's favorite color. However, through user feedback, I learned that the color scheme did not match the elegant and refined experience she envisioned. After multiple user feedback sessions, I decided to change the color scheme to a more sophisticated palette of ballerina pink and cream. This change not only enhanced the app's visual appeal but also aligned with Andrea's expectations of a refined and elegant experience.

Final Outcomes

The final design of Madame Coffee successfully combines elegance and functionality. The app's personalized coffee recommendations, along with positive affirmations, create a unique and engaging experience for users. The refined color scheme and user-friendly interface enhance the overall aesthetic, making it a delightful addition to any coffee lover's daily routine.
